## What is this?
Privacy-first feed curator for Bluesky & Mastodon.
Your browsing interests stay in YOUR browser only.
Zero server storage. 100% Open Source.

## Live App
👉 [my-curator.vercel.app](https://my-curator.vercel.app)

## Features
- 🔍 Google search tracking (local only)
- ▶️ YouTube watch history (local only)
- 🛒 Amazon & eBay shopping searches (local only)
- 🎬 Netflix viewing history (local only)
- 🎵 Spotify listening history (local only)
- 🎮 Twitch channels & games (local only)
- 🦋 Bluesky feed curation
- 🐘 Mastodon feed curation
- ✨ Auto Feed (no account needed)

## Privacy
All data stays in YOUR browser.
Nothing is sent to any server.
